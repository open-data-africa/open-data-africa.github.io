Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contribute a dataset?

Visit our Contribute page to submit your dataset. We'll review it within 5 business days and get back to you.

Can I request a specific dataset?

Yes! Use the feedback form above to request specific datasets. We'll work with our partners to make them available.

How do I report an issue with a dataset?

Use the feedback form to report broken links, outdated information, or data quality issues. We prioritize these reports.

Can I partner with Open Data Africa?

Absolutely! Email us at aimadeinafrica@giz.de to discuss partnership opportunities, collaborations, or sponsorships.

How often is the catalogue updated?

We update the catalogue monthly with new datasets. Major updates are announced via our social media channels.
