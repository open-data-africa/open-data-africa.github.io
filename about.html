About

Open Data Africa is a data accessibility initiative under the AI Made in Africa program. Our mission is to empower African businesses, researchers, and AI innovators by making high-quality, openly licensed datasets easily discoverable and usable. We believe that access to open and locally relevant data is a foundational step toward accelerating innovation, inclusive economic development, and responsible AI in Africa. Through this project, we aim to bridge data gaps and promote responsible data governance across sectors like health, agriculture, environment, and digital economy.
